When you log in to the JKUAT Inventory Management System as a user, you arrive at your personal dashboard. This is your home screen — it gives you a snapshot of your activity, quick access to the inventory catalogue, and the status of any stock requests you have submitted.

<Note>
  Your account is created by the system administrator. If you do not have login credentials, contact your JKUAT system administrator.
</Note>

## What You See on the Dashboard

The user dashboard has three main sections:

### Summary Cards

At the top of the dashboard, you will see cards showing:

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| **My Requests** | The total number of stock requests you have submitted |
| **Pending** | Requests awaiting admin approval |
| **Approved** | Requests that have been approved |
| **Rejected** | Requests that were not approved |

### Recent Requests

Below the summary cards, a table shows your five most recent stock requests with their current status. Click **View All Requests** to see your complete request history.

### Quick Links

A row of quick-link buttons at the bottom lets you jump directly to:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Browse Inventory" icon="boxes-stacked" href="/user/viewing-inventory">
    View all available stock items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Submit a Request" icon="paper-plane" href="/user/requests">
    Request an item from the inventory.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## User Navigation Menu

The navigation menu gives you access to all sections of the user portal:

| Menu Item | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | Your home screen and activity summary |
| Inventory | Browse and search available items |
| My Requests | View all your submitted requests and their status |
| Profile | Update your personal details and password |
| Logout | End your session |

## Responsive Design

The user portal works on any device. On mobile phones and tablets, the navigation menu collapses into a compact icon. All tables and cards adjust to fit your screen.

<Tip>
  Check your dashboard regularly to track the status of your pending requests. Approvals and rejections appear here as soon as the administrator acts on them.
</Tip>
