Categories and departments help you organise inventory items in the JKUAT Inventory Management System. Every item belongs to a category (e.g. Stationery, Electronics, Furniture) and is assigned to a department (e.g. Library, ICT, Registry). Managing these groupings keeps your inventory structured and your reports meaningful.

## Managing Categories

### Viewing Categories

From the admin navigation menu, click **Categories**. The page lists all existing categories with the number of items in each.

### Adding a New Category

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Category">
    On the Categories page, click **Add New Category**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Category Details">
    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Category Name | Short, descriptive name (e.g. "Stationery", "Lab Equipment") |
    | Description | Optional details about what belongs in this category |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save Category**. The new category is immediately available when adding or editing inventory items.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing a Category

1. Find the category in the list.
2. Click the **Edit** icon on its row.
3. Update the name or description.
4. Click **Update Category**.

### Deleting a Category

<Warning>
  You cannot delete a category that has inventory items assigned to it. Reassign or delete those items first, then delete the category.
</Warning>

To delete an empty category, click the **Delete** icon and confirm.

## Managing Departments

Departments represent the organisational units within JKUAT (e.g. ICT Department, Library, School of Engineering).

### Adding a Department

1. In the admin navigation, click **Departments** (within the Categories section or its own menu item).
2. Click **Add New Department**.
3. Enter the department name and optional description.
4. Click **Save Department**.

### Editing and Deleting Departments

- **Edit**: Click the Edit icon, update the name, click Update.
- **Delete**: Only possible when no users or inventory items are assigned to the department.

## Category and Department Usage

Categories and departments appear as dropdown options in:
- The **Add/Edit Item** form in Inventory Management
- The **Add/Edit User** form in User Management
- **Reports** — filter by category or department to generate targeted reports
- **User portal** — users can filter the inventory view by category

<Tip>
  Keep category names short and consistent. Inconsistent naming (e.g. "IT Equipment" vs "ICT Equipment") makes reports harder to read and filter.
</Tip>

## Recommended Category Structure for JKUAT

| Category | Example Items |
|---|---|
| Stationery | Pens, paper, folders, staplers |
| Electronics & ICT | Computers, projectors, cables, printers |
| Furniture | Chairs, desks, shelving units |
| Laboratory Equipment | Beakers, microscopes, reagents |
| Cleaning Supplies | Detergent, mops, bins |
| Medical Supplies | First-aid kits, gloves, sanitisers |
